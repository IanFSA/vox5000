Help & Guide

Everything you need to get the best out of Vox5000.

⚠ Use Google Chrome on a desktop or laptop. Mobile Safari and Firefox have limited support for the Web Audio and recording APIs that Vox5000 depends on. Chrome desktop gives the most reliable experience.
About audio quality: Vox5000 records using your browser's built-in WebM Opus codec. The WAV export option converts this recording to WAV format — it is not a true uncompressed PCM capture. For broadcast delivery, MP3 320 kbps or the WebM file both work well. If you need true lossless capture, record directly into your DAW using an audio interface.

🎙 Solo recording

  • Click Start solo recording on the home page
  • Select your microphone and choose MP3 or WAV export
  • Do the 5-second mic test to check your levels
  • Hit Record — the same button stops recording
  • Your file downloads automatically when you stop
  • Nothing is sent to any server — all recording is local
  • WAV export converts from the recorded audio — not a true lossless capture

🎧 Hosting an interview

  • Click Create interview room and name the room
  • Copy the guest invite link — send via WhatsApp or email
  • Do not use the guest link yourself — reopen your room from My Rooms
  • Admit guests from the waiting queue
  • Check consent status for each guest
  • Hit Record — 5-second countdown on all screens
  • Stop recording — guest tracks transfer to you automatically
  • Download separate tracks and the consent log
  • Tracks are WebM Opus files — open in any modern DAW or editor

🙋 Joining as a guest

  • Click the link the host sent you — no install needed
  • Put wired headphones on before entering
  • Select your mic and do the mic test on the setup screen
  • Enter your name — it labels your audio track
  • Choose whether to be recorded or join as Observer (mic off)
  • Wait for the host to admit you
  • Keep the tab open until you see the green Done message
  • If transfer fails, download your backup track and send it to the host

🔒 Consent & privacy

  • Solo recordings stay entirely on your device — nothing is sent anywhere
  • In interview rooms, audio transfers peer-to-peer — Vox5000 never sees it
  • The consent log is stored locally by the host and downloaded as a text file
  • The log includes name, role, consent status, timestamp and browser details
  • IP addresses are NOT collected or logged
  • Observer mode: mic off, can hear and chat, not recorded

🎚 Audio quality

  • External USB or XLR interface mic — not the laptop built-in
  • Wired headphones — not Bluetooth
  • Quiet room — close doors, turn off air conditioning
  • Levels peaking at –12 dB — not 0 dB
  • MP3 320 kbps for broadcast delivery and sharing
  • WebM Opus is the native recorded format — high quality at low file size
  • WAV export is a conversion — not original PCM
  • Chrome on desktop for interviews

💾 Downloads & formats

  • MP3 — compressed, great for delivery and sharing
  • WAV export — PCM WAV converted from the recorded audio
  • WebM — the native recorded format, opens in all modern editors
  • 320 kbps MP3 is the standard for radio delivery
  • Interview room tracks download as WebM Opus files
  • Guest tracks are labelled with the guest's name and a timestamp
  • Files go to your Downloads folder — Ctrl+J / Cmd+J in Chrome to find them

🔧 Troubleshooting

🚫 My microphone is not showing up
Make sure your mic is plugged in before loading the page. Check it appears in your computer's sound settings. Refresh after plugging it in. On Windows, check Chrome has mic permission in Settings → Privacy → Microphone.
🔒 I clicked Deny on the microphone permission
Click the padlock or microphone icon in Chrome's address bar → Site settings → Microphone → Allow. Then refresh the page.
🔊 I can hear myself echoing
You or your guest is using speakers instead of headphones. Speakers pick up the call audio and feed it back into the mic. Everyone must use wired headphones or earphones.
📊 The recording is too quiet or distorted
Too quiet — increase Input Gain until peaks hit around –12 dB. Distorted (red on waveform) — reduce Input Gain immediately. Target: –24 to –6 dB.
👥 Guests are not appearing in my waiting queue
Make sure you sent the correct guest invite link (copy it from the Invite Guests button — not from My Rooms). Both host and guest must have their pages open at the same time. Ask the guest to refresh and try again. Both should use Chrome on desktop.
📤 The guest transfer is stuck or not progressing
Both host and guest must keep their page open and not lock their screen. If the transfer stalls, the guest will be prompted to download their backup track and send it via WeTransfer or Google Drive.
🔗 My Rooms opens the room as a guest instead of host
Always use the Open as host button in My Rooms to reopen your room. Do not copy the guest link and open it yourself — that link is for guests only.
📱 I'm recording on a phone or tablet
Use Google Chrome on your device, allow microphone access, keep the Vox5000 tab open, and do not lock the screen or switch apps during recording.
💾 I can't find my downloaded file
Check your Downloads folder. In Chrome press Ctrl+J (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+J (Mac) to see recent downloads. Files include your name and a timestamp, e.g. Ian_HOST_2026-06-21.webm
🏠 My rooms have disappeared
Rooms are stored in your browser's local storage. They can be lost if you clear browser data, use private/incognito mode, or switch to a different browser or device.
