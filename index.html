How interview rooms work
1
Click Create interview room and enter your name
2
Copy the invite link and send it to guests via WhatsApp or email
3
Guests click the link, enter their name and consent to recording
4
Admit guests from your waiting queue
5
Hit Record — 5 second countdown on all screens, then everyone records locally
6
Stop the session — guest tracks upload to you automatically
7
Download separate WAV files clearly labelled with each person's name
For best broadcast quality
  • Always use wired headphones — Bluetooth adds latency and potential dropouts
  • Use an external USB or XLR interface mic — not your laptop's built-in mic
  • Keep the level meter peaking around –12 dB — never hitting 0 dB
  • If the waveform goes red, reduce input gain immediately
  • Monitor only affects what you hear — it does not change the recording
  • Do the 5s mic test before every session and check your sound
  • Use Chrome on a desktop or laptop for the most reliable experience
