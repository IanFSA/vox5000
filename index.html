No microphone was detected. Make sure your USB or XLR interface mic is plugged in and recognised by your computer, then refresh the page.

How interview rooms work

2
Send the link to your guests via WhatsApp or email
3
Guests join, consent to recording, and wait to be admitted
4
Hit Record — a 5 second countdown starts on all screens
5
Everyone records locally on their own device
6
Stop recording — guest tracks upload to the host automatically
7
Download separate WAV files, clearly labelled by name
For best broadcast quality
  • Always use wired headphones — Bluetooth adds latency and potential dropouts
  • Use an external USB or XLR interface mic — not your laptop's built-in mic
  • Keep the level meter peaking around –12 dB — never hitting 0 dB
  • If the waveform goes red, reduce input gain immediately
  • Monitor only affects what you hear — it does not change the recording
  • Do the 5s mic test before every session
  • Use Chrome on a desktop or laptop for the most reliable experience
