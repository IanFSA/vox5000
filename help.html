Solo recording

Click Start solo recording on the home page. Select your microphone and choose MP3 or WAV export. Do the 5 second mic test to check your levels. Hit Record — the same button stops recording. Your file downloads automatically when you stop.

Nothing is sent to any server. All recording is local to your device.

Note on WAV export: Vox5000 records using your browser's WebM Opus codec. The WAV export converts this to WAV format — it is not a true uncompressed PCM capture. For broadcast delivery, MP3 320 kbps works well. If you need true lossless capture, record directly into your DAW using an audio interface.

Hosting an interview

  • 1
    Click Create interview room and name the room
  • 2
    Copy the guest invite link and send via WhatsApp or email
  • 3
    Do not use the guest link yourself — reopen your room from My Rooms
  • 4
    Admit guests from the waiting queue and check consent status
  • 5
    Hit Record — a 5 second countdown syncs all screens
  • 6
    Stop recording — guest tracks transfer to you automatically
  • 7
    Download separate tracks and the consent log

Tracks are WebM Opus files — open in any modern DAW or editor.

🙋

Joining as a guest

  • 1
    Click the link the host sent you — no install needed
  • 2
    Put wired headphones on before entering
  • 3
    Select your mic and do the mic test on the setup screen
  • 4
    Enter your name — it labels your audio track
  • 5
    Choose whether to be recorded or join as Observer (mic off)
  • 6
    Wait for the host to admit you
  • 7
    Keep the tab open until you see the green Done message

If the transfer fails, download your backup track and send it to the host via WeTransfer or Google Drive.

Audio quality tips

Use an external USB or XLR interface mic — not the laptop built in. Use wired headphones, not Bluetooth. Record in a quiet room — close doors, turn off air conditioning. Keep levels peaking at minus 12 dB, not 0 dB. Use Chrome on desktop for interviews.

MP3 320 kbps is the standard for radio delivery. The native recorded format is WebM Opus — high quality at low file size and opens in all modern editors.

💾

Downloads and file formats

Format Best for
MP3 Broadcast delivery and sharing. 320 kbps. Small file size, universally compatible.
WAV Editing and archiving. Converted from recorded audio — not original PCM, but a clean working file.
WebM Native recorded format from interview rooms. Opens in all modern DAWs and editors. High quality, small size.

Guest tracks are labelled with the guest's name and a timestamp. Files go to your Downloads folder — press Ctrl+J or Cmd+Shift+J in Chrome to find recent downloads.

Troubleshooting

My microphone is not showing up

Make sure your mic is plugged in before loading the page. Check it appears in your computer's sound settings. Refresh after plugging it in. On Windows, check Chrome has mic permission in Settings → Privacy → Microphone.

I clicked Deny on the microphone permission

Click the padlock or microphone icon in Chrome's address bar → Site settings → Microphone → Allow. Then refresh the page.

I can hear myself echoing

You or your guest is using speakers instead of headphones. Speakers pick up the call audio and feed it back into the mic. Everyone must use wired headphones or earphones.

The recording is too quiet or distorted

Too quiet — increase Input Gain until peaks hit around minus 12 dB. Distorted (red on waveform) — reduce Input Gain immediately. Target: minus 24 to minus 6 dB.

Guests are not appearing in my waiting queue

Make sure you sent the correct guest invite link. Both host and guest must have their pages open at the same time. Ask the guest to refresh and try again. Both should use Chrome on desktop.

The guest transfer is stuck or not progressing

Both host and guest must keep their page open and not lock their screen. If the transfer stalls, the guest will be prompted to download their backup track and send it via WeTransfer or Google Drive.

My Rooms opens the room as a guest instead of host

Always use the Open as host button in My Rooms to reopen your room. Do not copy the guest link and open it yourself — that link is for guests only.

My rooms have disappeared

Rooms are stored in your browser's local storage. They can be lost if you clear browser data, use private or incognito mode, or switch to a different browser or device.
