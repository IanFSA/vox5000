Privacy

Your audio never leaves your device.

Vox5000 was built on a simple principle: your recordings belong to you and nobody else.

The short version: Solo recordings are entirely local to your device. In interview rooms, audio transfers peer to peer between participants. Vox5000 never stores, processes, or has access to any of your audio.

We do not run advertising. We do not sell data. We do not track you across other sites.

Audio and recordings

Solo recordings are captured entirely within your browser using the Web Audio API. No audio data is sent to any server at any point. The file is written directly to your device when you stop recording.

Interview room recordings work the same way for each participant. Every person records locally on their own device. At the end of a session, guest tracks are transferred directly to the host using a peer to peer connection. Vox5000's servers are not involved in this transfer and never receive, store, or process any audio.

Consent logs

When guests join an interview room, they are asked to confirm consent to being recorded. A consent log is generated locally by the host and downloaded as a text file at the end of the session. This log is stored on the host's device only. Vox5000 does not retain copies of consent logs.

The log includes each participant's name as entered, their role (host or guest), consent status, timestamp, and browser information.

IP addresses are not collected or logged.

Room data

Interview rooms are stored in your browser's local storage on your device. Room data includes the room name, creation date, and guest invite link. This data never leaves your browser and is not synced to any server. Clearing your browser data or using incognito mode will remove saved rooms.

Website analytics

Vox5000 may use privacy respecting analytics to understand general usage patterns such as which pages are visited and how long sessions last. No personally identifiable information is collected. No individual user profiles are built. No data is sold or shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

Newsletter

If you subscribe to the Vox5000 newsletter, your email address is stored by Beehiiv, our newsletter platform. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any newsletter email. We do not share your email address with any third party.

Cookies

Vox5000 uses only functional cookies necessary for the site to operate. We do not use advertising cookies or third party tracking cookies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how Vox5000 handles your data, you can reach us at hello@vox5000.com.
