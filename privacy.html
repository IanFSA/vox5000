Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026 · Operated by Vox5000 · South Africa

The short version: Vox5000 does not record, store, or transmit your audio. Everything you record stays on your own device. We do not know who you are.

Who we are

Vox5000 (vox5000.com) is a free browser-based audio recording tool built for radio presenters, journalists, podcasters, and voice over artists. The service is operated from South Africa and is subject to the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

What we do not collect

Vox5000 is designed to collect as little information as possible. We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your audio recordings — these are created and saved entirely on your own device
  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your microphone input or any audio signal
  • Your IP address beyond what your browser sends to any web server as a normal part of loading a page
  • Cookies for tracking or advertising purposes

How recording works

When you use Vox5000, your browser requests access to your microphone. This permission is handled entirely by your browser and your operating system — Vox5000 never receives or stores your audio on any server. All recording, encoding, and file creation happens locally in your browser. When you click download, the file goes directly from your browser to your device. No audio data ever leaves your computer.

Microphone permission

Your browser will ask you to grant microphone access before you can record. You can revoke this permission at any time in your browser settings. Vox5000 only uses microphone access while the recorder is open in your browser tab.

Website analytics

We may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how many people visit vox5000.com and which pages are most used. If we do, we will use a tool that does not use cookies and does not track individuals across websites. We will update this policy to name any analytics tool we add.

Google AdSense

Vox5000 may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's ad settings. Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-party services

Vox5000 loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log your IP address when your browser requests font files. This is standard browser behaviour and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. No other third-party services are loaded by default.

When you use the MP3 export feature, the lamejs encoding library is loaded from a content delivery network (cdnjs.cloudflare.com). Cloudflare may log your IP address as part of serving this file. The audio encoding itself happens entirely in your browser.

Children

Vox5000 is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Your rights under POPIA

As a South African operator, we are subject to the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA). Because we do not collect personal information about our users, there is in practice very little personal information for us to hold, correct, or delete. If you believe we hold any personal information about you and wish to request access, correction, or deletion, you may contact us using the details below.

Your rights under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation including the right to access, correct, or erase personal data we hold about you. Because we do not collect personal data beyond basic server logs, these rights are limited in practice. Contact us if you have any questions.

Data retention

We do not store your audio or personal data on our servers. Standard web server access logs, if any, are retained for no more than 30 days and are used only for security and performance monitoring.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of Vox5000 after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how Vox5000 handles data, please contact us at: privacy@vox5000.com
